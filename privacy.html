Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 14, 2026

Overview

Pulse Strength Club ("Pulse", "we", "us") respects your privacy. This Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices. It applies to our website and related services.

Information We Collect

  • Contact details: name, email address, phone number when you book a class or submit a contact form.
  • Booking data: selected class, day/time, and attendance history.
  • Usage data: pages visited, interactions, approximate location (via analytics).
  • Communications: messages you send to us for support or inquiries.

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and manage class bookings and membership services.
  • Send confirmations and important updates about your bookings.
  • Improve our website and services through aggregated analytics.
  • Respond to inquiries and provide customer support.

Cookies and Analytics

We use cookies and Google Analytics to understand site usage and improve performance. You can control cookies via your browser settings.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with trusted providers (e.g., email delivery, hosting, database) solely to operate our services and only under appropriate safeguards.

Data Retention

We retain your information as long as needed to provide services and meet legal obligations. You may request deletion of your data (subject to lawful requirements).

Your Rights

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information.
  • Opt-out of non-essential communications.
  • Request details about how we process your data.

To exercise these rights, contact us at contact@pulsestrength.club.

Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no online service can be completely secure.

Children

Our services are not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.

Contact

Questions? Email contact@pulsestrength.club or use the Get in Touch form on our homepage.
