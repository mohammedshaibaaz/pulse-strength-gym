Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior gym experience to join?

No. Our certified coaches work with members of all levels. We focus on teaching proper form and scaling exercises to your current ability, ensuring safe progression regardless of experience.

What's the difference between membership tiers?

Basic includes 24/7 gym access. Pro adds group classes and nutrition guidance. Elite includes personal coaching with custom program design. Choose based on your coaching needs and schedule.

Can I cancel my membership anytime?

Yes. We offer month-to-month membership with no long-term contracts. Cancel anytime through your member portal or contact us directly.

How do you track progress?

Members get access to our tracking app where we log workouts, measure strength gains, and adjust programs based on real performance data and goals.

Is there a free trial?

Yes. Start with a 7-day free trial to experience our facility, meet our coaches, and try a training session. No credit card required.

What equipment do you have?

Full selection of power racks, squat stands, benches, dumbbells (5-140kg), barbells, functional training rigs, cable machines, and specialized strength equipment.
